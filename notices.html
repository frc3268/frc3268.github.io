Due to a lack of public funding, there will be no activity bus this year. We are still going to be holding meetings as previously scheduled, so the upcoming meeting on Monday, 9/9, will occur.

Reefscape 2025 Kicks Off

The 2025 FRC season kicked off on January 4, 2025, with a meeting at inglemoor to mark the event. The game, Reefscape, combines pick-and-place with shooter play, though with a heavy emphasis on the former. The upcoming meeting, on Monday, January 6, will be a late night in order to continue design work and begin our build season.
